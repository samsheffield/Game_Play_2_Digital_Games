CHALLENGES IN GAMES

Some questions for you...

  • What is the role of challenge in games?
  • Is challenge the same as difficulty?
  • Can easy games be challenging?
  • How do we design interesting challenges?

INTERESTING CHOICES

Games are a series of interesting decisions. - Sid Meier, of Firaxis Games
  • What does this mean?
  • Does this describe all games?

SOME CHARACTERISTICS OF INTERESTING DECISIONS IN STRATEGIC PLAY

  1. Involve a tradeoff (Risk vs. reward)
  2. Situational (Interesting interaction with systems)
  3. Expressive or Creative (Encourage personal approach/play style)
  4. Persistent (Consequences that players understand)
  5. Payoff (Sense of accomplishment)

MAKING STRATEGIC DECISIONS MORE INTERESTING

This requires balancing constraints... More vs. Less.

  1. Quantity of choices.
  2. Quality/importance of choices.
  3. Availability of information.
  4. Time.
  5. Creativity & self-discovery.
