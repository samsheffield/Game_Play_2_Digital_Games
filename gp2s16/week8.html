GAME BALANCE

In game design, balance refers to the tuning of a game's mechanics to identify and fix innefective or problematic elements.

  • Game balancing is not an exact science (more like a black art!)
  • Game balancing is only useful if the core mechanics of your game are already in place.
  • Game balance is often done through playtesting, but you can also rely on your instincts as a game designer. (MATH works well for particular games with complex cost/benefit ratios)

Game Balancing Methodologies... (It's all about the feels!)

  • Is the challenge in my level feel appropriate?
  • Does one strategy feel better or worse than any other?
  • Does my level feel innately complex, or does the complexity emerge from simple mechanics?
