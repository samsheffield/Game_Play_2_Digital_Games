HOMEWORK

PLATFORMER PROTOTYPE

Create a single platforming level which pits the player against the environment (don't focus on player vs. enemy conflict on this one.) In addtion, include a player which has 3 states of animation (example: idle, walk, jump) that are triggered through input.

Compelling level design involves the thoughful manipulation of a player's movement through space. Experiment with the arrangement of platform elements in your level to inform, confound, or challenge the player. Feel free to add a narrative element to your level, though make it secondary to the level's design.
