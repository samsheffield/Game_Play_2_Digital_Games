TIPS ON TEAMWORKING

Working in teams can be challenging for a number of reasons. With that in mind, here are some things to consider:

  • Communicate: Be on the same page. Be honest. Don't assume. WRITE IT DOWN.
  • Be objective: Table feelings & personal preferences. Focus on discussing well-designed solutions to problems.
  • Be respectful: Trust is earned. Every voice matters. Discuss & listen.
  • Unite: Come to an agreement that allows you to keep moving forward. Disagree and commit. Let things go.
