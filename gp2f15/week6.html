PROJECT 1: SCORE-BASED GAME (DUE 10/28)

For the first project, you will be responsible for completing a single level, score-based game for one player. Your focus will be on on designing a gameplay experience that is challenging, fun, and can be played repleatedly for score. The theme, mechanics, and scoring system of the game are completely up to you, as long as they work with the requirements listed below.

Everyone will work in small groups to design, prototype, and develop a game which will be presented at the start of class on the 28th. The workload must be shared equally between all team members. It will be your collective responsibility to delegate tasks (who will code what parts, who will handle visual assets, who will be responsible for sound, etc.) At the end of the project, each team member will responsible for completing a peer review to provide feedback on his/her partners.

REQUIREMENTS

  • One player only.
  • One highly polished game level (no more!).
  • Game must have an end condition.
  • A scoring system which encourages replayability.
  • Controls limited to: directional movement controlled by a joystick/gamepad, 2 input buttons, 1 start button (no more! no mouse!)
  • Must include the following scenes (at minimum): Start, Game, End
  • Complete instructions somehow include in-game.
  • A Cohesive and memorable concept established through sound, visuals, and animation.
  • Unique assets (no unlicensed sound or visuals)
